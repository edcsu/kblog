Tuesday was Worker's day which is a public holiday that we respected and we rested.

I gave a snippet on the fourth habit mentioned in the book The 7 Habits Of Highly Effective People written by Stephen R. Covey which is "Think Win-Win". It's a princple of interpaersonal leadership For it to work the following have to be achieved:-

  1. My character has to be built on integrity, maturity and abundant mentality.
  2. Have relationships based on trust and positive cooperative energy.
  3. The relationships should be based on agreements that are defined and directly lead to win-win.
  4. Engage in win-win trainings.
  5. Then perform win-win tasks.

We talked about presenting feedback using the Sandwich method. First I praise the recepient, then give constructive critiscism and finally give them a target to undertake.

Also I got to know 16 negotiating do's that I must undergo before I get to the negotiation table as per Dr. Tim Henning.
