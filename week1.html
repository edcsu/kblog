Week 1

It began on Monday 26th. First up was the leadership course which stretched all my imaginations. It was an eye opener to the way I view leadership that it's enshrined in all of us and being a leadership position doesn't mean it's leadership. Later we drew out a career development plan personally that we are going to follow through in the course and later on i.e long term goals . Later on in the day, we were introduced to software development platforms and HTML5 not forgetting CSS. I got exposed to Microsoft Visual Code Studio that simplified my coding experience and local hosting with XAMP just enlighted me further in web development.

Mid-week we talked about change and how we react to it. The four polar axes;

  • Incremental
  • Radical
  • Focused
  • Flexible
help us to find out how we react to problems. I doing so, we have to take up these roles;
  • Visionary
  • Strategist
  • Planner
  • Practioner
to become effective leaders.

Can't wait for next week.
